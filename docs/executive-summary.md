
Overview

This repository documents a structured IT General Controls (ITGC) assessment of a Jira change management system supporting regulated operational delivery. The assessment aligns with SOX Section 404 expectations and enterprise technology risk governance standards.

The objective was to evaluate access controls, change authorization workflows, segregation of duties, audit trail integrity, and evidence retention practices within the Jira environment.

Control Domains Assessed
Access Governance (User & Role Management)
Change Management & Approval Controls
Segregation of Duties
Audit Logging & Traceability
Evidence Retention & Documentation Integrity
Assessment Approach
Defined control objectives
Built structured control matrix
Designed sampling methodology
Conducted simulated testing procedures
Identified control deficiencies
Developed remediation recommendations
Evaluated financial and operational risk impact
Business Impact

This assessment demonstrates applied understanding of SOX ITGC control evaluation, risk quantification, audit defensibility, and enterprise governance alignment.
